Top Destinations

Wisata Buken

Wisata Buken offers beautiful views of the hills and rice fields, complete with a swimming pool. An ideal place to relax and enjoy nature.

Embung Banteran

Embung Banteran offers beautiful views of Mount Slamet, with ATVs, water bikes and a children's play area. Enjoy delicious culinary delights at the restaurants around this artificial lake.

Taman Panemon

Taman Panemon has a beautiful swimming pool that gives off an ocean-like feel, ideal for relaxing and enjoying nature.
